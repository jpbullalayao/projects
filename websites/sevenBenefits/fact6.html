Exercising with Xbox Kinect

6 - Promoting Exercise

Video games these days isn't just about sitting in front of an HDTV all day mashing buttons on your controller. And it hasn't been for several years now. Video games can now provide a viable option for exercising. Of course, this doesn't apply to all games, it only applies to those games that require the use of the Xbox Kinect, Playstation Move and Nintendo Wii.

Nowadays, people of all ages can enjoy exercising with the Xbox Kinect, Playstation Move and Ninteod Wii. These three provide viable options for exercising, and it's also great because you are almost guaranteed to have fun while playing these exercise games. These are one of the greatest benefits that video games nowadays can provide for the gamers. Many people are using these games as a way to help stay fit and healthy while also exercising outside. The last benefit I will be going into is confidence.
